Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Tic Tac Toe Quest, a single-player offline mobile game. The app is designed not to collect personal data.

Data Collection

Tic Tac Toe Quest does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Local Game Data

The app stores gameplay progress and sound settings locally on your device. This may include unlocked levels, level stars, best moves, best times, and music or sound effect preferences. This information stays on your device and is not sent to the developer or any third party.

No Accounts or Online Services

The app does not require user accounts, login, registration, or online synchronization.

No Advertising or Tracking

The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party data collection tools.

No In-App Purchases

The app does not include in-app purchases or subscriptions.

Children's Privacy

Because the app does not collect personal data, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

If the app changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated before or alongside the release of that change.

Contact

For privacy questions, please use the developer support contact listed on the App Store page for Tic Tac Toe Quest.
