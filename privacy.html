Privacy Policy

21 Card Clear - Last updated: June 10, 2026

21 Card Clear is designed as an offline game. The app does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. 21 Card Clear does not require an account, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, microphone access, camera access, or any other personal identifier.

Local Game Data

The app may store gameplay progress locally on your device, including unlocked levels, star ratings, best scores, last played level, and music or sound effect settings. This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or to a third party.

Network Access

21 Card Clear is a standalone offline game. It does not use a server connection for gameplay, analytics, advertising, remote configuration, or account services.

Advertising and Analytics

The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or social login SDKs.

Purchases

The app does not include in-app purchases, subscriptions, paid virtual items, gambling, betting, cash rewards, or real-money mechanics.

Children's Privacy

Because the app does not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children or from any other users.

Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties because we do not collect user data.

Data Deletion

You can delete locally stored game progress by deleting the app from your device. Reinstalling the app may reset local progress and settings depending on your device and operating system behavior.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the App Store product page.
